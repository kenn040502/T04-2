About this project

Purpose

Supports COS30045 T01/T02 assessments. T01(a): hosted site & layout; T01(b): KNIME data processing; T02: charts responding to Q1–Q6.

Data sources & limitations

  • TV energy data: Australian Government Energy Rating database (unit practical snapshot).
  • Units: active power (W); annual energy (kWh/year) where shown.
  • Aggregations: means by technology/size; results vary by brand/model features.
  • Limitations: dataset may lag newest models; usage patterns not captured.
